About Magic Gear

Magic Gear is an innovative tactical espionage game that combines stealth, strategy, and compelling storytelling. Follow the journey of Pax, a pacifist agent who must navigate dangerous situations while staying true to her principles.

The Story

In a world where conflict seems inevitable, agent Pax stands as a beacon of hope. Armed with her wit, advanced technology, and unwavering commitment to non-violence, she embarks on a mission to rescue her captured allies and prevent the activation of the devastating Magic Gear weapon.

Every decision matters in this game where intelligence triumphs over brute force, and where the greatest victories are achieved without firing a single shot.
